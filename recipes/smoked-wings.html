Smoked Wings

Description:

These delicious, crispy, juicy smoked wings are sure to wow everybody at your next backyard barbecue!

Ingredients:

  • Your favorite brand of Chicken Wings
  • My All Purpose BBQ Rub
  • My Honey Habanero BBQ Sauce
    • or your choice of sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt (for dry brining)

Steps:

  1. Pat the wings dry with a paper towel, generously sprinkle with salt and place in the refrigerator uncovered for 4-8 hours
  2. After brining is finished, remove the wings from the refrigerator, stir together the baking powder and rub in a bowl and toss the wings in the mixture, stir until evenly coated
  3. Preheat your smoker to 250 degrees F, place your wings on a baking sheet and smoke for 2 hours
  4. Remove the wings from the smoker and crank it up to 400 degrees F. As we wait for the smoker to reach temp, brush the wings with olive oil and place them back on the smoker when ready
  5. When the wings reach the correct internal temperature (180-200 degrees F), begin to brush them with the bbq sauce every few minutes until you are ready to eat
